# 🏦 Banking Customer Analytics Dashboard

End-to-End Data Analytics project that analyzes banking customer behavior using Python and Power BI. The project provides valuable insights into customer financial behavior, loan exposure, deposit trends, banking relationships, and customer engagement to support data-driven banking and business decisions.

## 🚀 Features

✅ Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

✅ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

✅ Statistical Analysis

✅ Correlation Analysis

✅ Feature Engineering

✅ Banking KPI Analysis

✅ Loan Analysis

✅ Deposit Analysis

✅ Customer Engagement Analysis

✅ Interactive Power BI Dashboard

✅ Business Insights & Recommendations

## 📊 Core Analysis Modules

### Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Performed comprehensive data exploration, descriptive statistics, missing value analysis, and feature engineering to understand customer financial behavior.

### Loan Analysis

Analyzes total loans, bank loans, business lending, and credit card balances across different customer segments.

### Deposit Analysis

Examines customer deposits including bank deposits, savings accounts, checking accounts, and foreign currency accounts.

### Customer Segmentation

Analyzes customers based on banking relationship, gender, occupation, nationality, income band, institution advisor, and year of joining.

### Correlation Analysis

Evaluates relationships between banking products to identify customer financial behavior patterns and cross-selling opportunities.

### Dashboard Analytics

Interactive multi-page Power BI dashboard featuring KPIs, filters, charts, and banking performance metrics.

---

## 📈 Key Results

* Analyzed banking data of approximately **3,000 customers** across multiple financial attributes.
* Identified a **strong positive correlation (0.84)** between **Bank Deposits** and **Checking Accounts**.
* Found a **strong positive correlation (0.75)** between **Bank Deposits** and **Savings Accounts**.
* Observed that higher-income customers tend to utilize multiple banking products.
* Developed an interactive **4-page Power BI dashboard** for banking performance monitoring.
* Built **12+ DAX measures** for dynamic KPI reporting and financial analysis.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Customers with higher bank deposits also maintain significantly higher checking account balances (**Correlation = 0.84**).
* Customers with larger deposits tend to maintain higher savings account balances (**Correlation = 0.75**).
* Higher-income customers generally utilize multiple banking products and services.
* No significant negative correlations were observed, indicating banking products complement one another.
* Long-term customers demonstrate higher engagement and stronger banking relationships.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

* Cross-sell savings and checking accounts to high-deposit customers.
* Introduce premium banking services for high-income customers.
* Develop customer loyalty programs to improve engagement.
* Use banking relationship insights for personalized financial products.
* Leverage dashboard analytics to support customer relationship management and strategic business decisions.
